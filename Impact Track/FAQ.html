Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some of the questions we hear the most from donors and the charities listed and/or rated on our site.

Does it cost to use Impact Track? +

No, you do not have to pay to use our service. We refuse to charge our users for trusted data and do not accept funding from the charities we evaluate, ensuring our ratings remain objective.

The CEO's salary of a charity seems high; should I still support them? +

Nonprofit CEO salaries vary for many reasons, including geography and organization size. Compensation is included in the expense ratios, which measure how a nonprofit allocates its budget towards its mission.

What should I do if I believe a charity is acting illegally or unethically? +

If you believe a charity is acting unethically or illegally, we encourage you to report your concerns to the appropriate authorities, such as the IRS or the Attorney General.
